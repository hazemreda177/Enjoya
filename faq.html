Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our experiences

What should I bring to the game?

All you need is your excitement and curiosity! We recommend wearing comfortable clothes and shoes suitable for walking. Any additional tools or materials required for the game (like maps, clues, or props) will be provided by us.

Can I customize a game for my team or event?

Absolutely! We specialize in designing tailored experiences for groups, whether it's for corporate team-building, birthdays, or special events. Just let us know your preferences, and we'll create an adventure that fits your needs.

Is there an age limit for participation?

Our games are designed for participants aged 13 and above. However, we can customize games for younger players if it's a family event. For horror-themed games, we recommend a minimum age of 16.

How long does each game last?

Most of our games are designed to last between 2 to 3 hours, depending on the complexity and the group's pace. Custom durations can be arranged for special events.

Are the games physically demanding?

Our games involve light walking and exploring, but they are not physically intense. We ensure the activities are accessible for most participants. If you have specific requirements, please inform us in advance.

What happens if I get stuck on a puzzle?

No worries! Our game facilitators will be available to provide hints or guidance if your team gets stuck. We want you to enjoy the challenge while keeping the experience fun and engaging.

Can we play as a team?

Yes! Our games are designed for teamwork. You can join as a group (ideal team size: 3â€“6 players) or be paired with others to enhance the interactive experience.

What if the weather is bad?

Most of our games are held outdoors, but we monitor the weather closely. In case of bad weather, we'll either reschedule or provide an alternative indoor experience.

How do I book a game?

Booking is easy! Just fill out the form on our website with your name, phone number, and preferred game. Our team will contact you to confirm your booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 48 hours before the game. For cancellations within 48 hours, a partial refund may be provided based on the circumstances.

How much does it cost to participate?

Prices vary depending on the game and group size:

  • Individual bookings start from EGP 350 per person
  • Corporate packages and group events start from EGP 7,000 per session

Do I need prior experience to play?

Not at all! Our games are designed for everyone, from beginners to experienced adventurers. We provide clear instructions at the start, so you'll know exactly what to do.

How do you ensure safety during the games?

Your safety is our top priority. All games are supervised by trained facilitators, and we choose safe, well-maintained locations. In case of any emergencies, our team is always ready to assist.

Can I book a private game for my group?

Yes, you can! Private games are perfect for birthdays, family outings, or corporate team-building. Just let us know your group size and preferred date, and we'll arrange everything for you.
